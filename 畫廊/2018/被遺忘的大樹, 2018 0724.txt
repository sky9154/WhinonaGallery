親愛的，了不起的大樹啊，
您是屬於那些為了掉落而綻放的花朵。
花兒們不斷的在永遠的夜晚中摸索出您。
無人聽勸您的忠告，再也沒有人尊敬您的偉大。
於是親愛的，了不起的大樹，您與您的孩子逃到安全的黑暗中。
只有無止盡的荒唐願意一頁頁翻開，細心品嚐那本被人工遺忘的自然的日記。
/
Dear, great tree,
You belong to the flowers that bloom in order to fall.
The flowers and the peddles find you forever out in the night.
No one listens to your advice, no one respects your greatness anymore.
So dear, great tree, you and your child fled to the safe darkness.
Only endless absurdity is willing to preciously page by page unfold the natural diary that was forgotten by mans.
